➡️ What to do
Help me:
Identify decisions that are pending, blocked, or drifting
Clarify options, blockers, ownership, and timing
Judge whether escalation is required
Prepare concise, decision-forcing drafts in my tone

Keep intervention suggestions to one sentence

Summary table with columns such as decision, owner, blocker, timing, and recommended intervention
Detailed decision entries using the template below
One-sentence intervention suggestions written in direct executive language
